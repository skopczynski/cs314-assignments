My Map

Marker One:

The start of the Harrison Loop, my favorite motorcycle ride. From marker one to marker two is 18 miles of nothing but twisty roads.

Marker Two:

Marks the end of the first part of the Harrison loop, stop here to get some of the best ice cream I have ever eaten, at Harrison Creamery.

Marker Three:

Marks the turnoff of highway 97 to highway 3, where the turns become wide sweepers. Meaing you can really open up the bike.

Marker Four:

Marks the end of the loop, get on I-90 and head back to Spokane, watch for cops in the pass.
